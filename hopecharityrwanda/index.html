About Us

Contribution is a humanitarian project born in Kigali, focused on emergency support, safe housing advocacy, and community resilience. Our work centers on vulnerable communities, including those affected by seasonal flooding and homelessness, ensuring children and adults have safe living spaces and dignity.

Our Story

English Version

Seasonal heavy rainfall has repeatedly caused flooding in Nyabugogo, one of Kigali’s most flood-prone areas. Public news reports have documented injuries, loss of life, and evacuations during these floods.

Homelessness remains a serious challenge in areas such as Gatenga, Kimonyi, and Nyamagumba, where children and adults live without stable shelter. These conditions require urgent action to ensure safe housing, access to food, healthcare, and education.

French Version

Les fortes pluies saisonnières ont provoqué à plusieurs reprises des inondations à Nyabugogo, une zone très exposée aux crues à Kigali. Les médias publics ont rapporté des blessés, des décès et des évacuations liés à ces inondations.

Le sans-abrisme reste un problème majeur dans des zones comme Gatenga, Kimonyi, et Nyamagumba, où des enfants et des adultes vivent sans logement stable. Ces conditions nécessitent une action urgente pour assurer un logement sûr, l'accès à l'alimentation, aux soins de santé et à l'éducation.

Why We Act

These situations highlight urgent needs: safer infrastructure, emergency response, and support for affected families. Contribution mobilizes donors and partners to reduce risk and provide help when disasters strike or people are living without shelter.
